welcome to keybear

keybear is a single sign-on identity service that works offline without servers using public key cryptography.
Your identity is just the hash of your public key.

Sorry, your browser doesn't support web crypto. You will need at least:
  • Internet Explorer 11
  • Firefox 35
  • Chrome 37
  • Safari { Desktop: 7.1, iOS: 8 }
  • Opera 25

This service is an experimental proof of concept, so don't rely on it for anything sensitive or important. For unimportant things, go wild!

This application is distributed with hyperboot. After the first use, the contents of this page are frozen at this version. You can opt-in to upgrades by clicking the gear at the upper right.
